Seagull steals entire Greggs order on Park Street 'in broad daylight'

The brazen bird swooped on a full bag of pasties and made off without a backward glance – leaving stunned onlookers with one burning question

By Bristol City Centre Reporter · 11:45, 16 JUN 2026

A seagull has brazenly stolen an entire Greggs order on Park Street "in broad daylight", witnesses say, swooping in the moment the customer turned to grab a napkin.

The bird is reported to have made off with two sausage rolls, a steak bake and, most audaciously of all, the customer's coffee – before disappearing over the rooftops without so much as a backward glance.

"It was so calm about the whole thing," said one shaken onlooker. "It didn't even rush. It just took the bag like it had ordered ahead."

The victim, who had queued for several minutes, was said to be "more impressed than angry".

Asked by a passer-by how on earth the bird had paid for the lot, one onlooker simply shrugged.

"It just put it on the bill."

Greggs staff are yet to comment on whether the order will be refunded. The seagull remains at large.
